Fixed Deposit Opening through PhoneBanking
 
Background:-
HDFC Bank offers the customer simple and reinvestment fixed deposit, which can be opened with minimum investment of Rs..5000/- and make additions to their deposits in multiples of Rs.5000/- each. Reinvestment deposit can be invested for minimum of period of 6 month.
 
  1. Requisites:-
  2. Facility available only through agent services at PhoneBanking
  3. Mandatory Cust ID+TIN / Debit Card number+ PIN based validation.
  4. Services available to Level 3 TIN holders only
  5. FD can be opened at any branch of the Bank.
  6. FD rates to be offered as per existing applicable rates (To be checked from HDFC Bank site)
  7. Interest  payouts
  8. Interest payouts can be monthly or quarterly or reinvestment
  9. FD booked for upto 180 days - Simple fixed deposits
  10. FD booked for 180 days or more – to be booked as reinvestment deposits.
  11. FD booked for 180 days and above where interest payout is quarterly / monthly – to be booked as simple FD
  12. If the FD is booked at a non premium rate, (i.e at base rate = Less than 15lacs rate), then customer can opt for monthly interest pay-out option. Quarterly pay-out is allowed on premium rate FDs. The interest rate for monthly interest pay-out is always at a discounted rate.
  13. Tenure and Initial Deposit
  14. Minimum tenure :- 7 days for Simple Deposit and > 6 months for reinvestment deposit
  15. Maximum tenure :- 5 years for simple as well as reinvestment deposit
  16. Initial Deposit :- Rs.5000/- for Simple as well as reinvestment.
  17. Sweep-In facility to FDs through PhoneBanking
  18. TheFD  to be opened  inthe  single  name ofthe  caller  who identifies  himselfwith  the  Cust ID & TIN
  19. Sweep-in linkage is done  only to theCASA Account used for funding  the FD
  20. Request for linkage as Sweep-In is given at the time of opening the FD
  21. Sweep-in is permitted for fixed deposit of value >= 1 crore (base rate FDs only & non preferential tenor FDs) wherein the tenor is >=6 month 1 day. This is w.e.f.12th July'11
  22. Sweep in facility is not available on FD's booked at a premium rate for the preferential tenure

  2. In case FD is to be booked under “SC” category request to be taken only if the customer has an existing Saving under the SC category (Y” / “Z” category) . (product codes for SC  Fixed Deposits  336,441,573,593)


  1. Facilities offered /Not offered for FD booking through PhoneBanking
Facilities offered Facilities  not offered
  • FD in single name
  • FD in a  name of all joint applicants as per the   funding Account 
  • Sweep in facility (if   FD opened in single name)
  • FD can be opened at any   branch of the Bank.
  • FD rates offered are as   per existing applicable rates
  • NRE FD rates applicable   as per existing applicable rates
  • Sweep in facility   offered for FD booked in single name through PhoneBanking. Sweep in to be   linked to funding A/c. Request to be accepted on the same day as FD booked.

  1. Process of Capturing FD requests at PhoneBanking
   Process at agent Desk
  1. On receipt of customer call stating “Wish to / want to open a new Fixed deposit, below mentioned details to be checked before accepting the new Fixed Deposit Opening requests
  2. Validation Status
    • Agent to check the validation status of the applicant in RAP, customer has to validated basis Cust ID+TIN or Debit Card + PIN. If customer is not validated agent to validate the customer on IVR
    • Proprietorship Current A/c, agent to validate the Cust ID + TIN or Debit Card + PIN of the authorized signatory (Relationship "TRU"), On successful validation , capture the Fixed Deposit opening requests under Proprietorship Firms Cust ID
    • Beside the TIN/ PIN validation, agent to carryout one more Manual verification (Basis challenged questions)  to check the Identity of the customer.
  3. Check whether customer wish to open FD a/c with Single name or joint Name
AS PER THE ACCOUNT OPENING PATTERN
Customer Debit account number with currency as INR having status as “Regular” or “No credit” and where the customer has either a SOW, JOF, JOO, TRU or VAL relationship can only book the FD through RAP.
  • FD can be opened in a single name of the caller or in joint names of all the applicants in the funding Account., Operating instructions details mentioned below,
    • Single :- SOW
    • Joint :- JOO(joint or other) or JOF (joint or first) E/S (Either or Survivor) or A/S (Anyone or Survivor)
    •  In case of customer wants to open joint FD, check the joint holder details, joint FD can be opened in the name of all applicants of the funding Account




  1. PAN number
If Booking amount is > = 50,000/- and if PAN number is not updated in FC for the 1st holder of the debit account, then such FD requests will not get accepted in RAP
For FD request < Rs.50,000/- even when PAN is not present in FC, request will be accepted and processed. There is a system control in FC on controlling cases where the FD aggregate is > = 5 Lacs and where there is No PAN the same would get applied to FD request at PhoneBanking as well.


  1. Email ID and Registered mailing address for sending FD advice
    • Email ID :- Check the Email statement registration status of the customer to send the FD Advice (FC Fast path CIM09)
    • Registered mailing address :- If customer is not registered for E-mail statement, Customer to confirm the registered mailing address on Bank's record, (request to be accepted subject to registered mailing address on records accurately confirmed by customer)
  2. Capture the FD request in RAP
On successful scrutiny, agent to capture details in RAP / FD Annexure








  • Post submitting the request, RAP will populate the FD details for Reconfirmation, agent to check and submit the request


  • Agent to reconfirm (read back) the FD details updated in RAP/annexure to ensure correct logging of FD request and inform the customer that the FD request has been logged successfully


  • Value date for FD booking will be the day on which the request was accepted in RAP


  • IMP – On month      ends units to ensure that there are no requests taken after 17.30 hrs.      Incase of any FD requests at month end post cut off of 17.30 hrs customers      to be diverted to NetBanking for FD booking. No request to be taken at      PhoneBanking. in case there are any requests that are taken after the cut      off time during the month end, the supervisor verifying the request should      reject the request in front end (RAP Admin).








  1. Details to be informed to the customer :-
    • Inform Funding a/c will be debited within 24 hours
    • TAT :- 
      • If Register for E-mail Statement :-  3 Working Days to receive the FD advise in soft form
      • If  customer is not registered for E-mail Statement :-  7 working Days to receive the physical FD Advise
  2. In case of Sweep Out facility in Saving MAX a/c :- If customer is registered for Email statement, Email advices to be sent to the customer & if not registered for E-mail statement, no physical advices to be sent to the customer
  3. Interest payment and redemption of FD amount on maturity will be made only to the funding account.
  4. Value Date is current date (FD request date).


   Process at Supervisor Desk
  1. Supervisor to Authorized the request in RAP admin module (RAP location) / Physical Annexure (Non RAP location) post checking the following details.
    1. Check the TIN/PIN validation status & Level of TIN in Base-24 option "ITLF"
      1. For Proprietorship a/c, check the TIN validation status of Authorized Signatory (Relation ship "TRU") and FD opening requested for Proprietorship Company

  3. No of Joint holders in the account
  4. Sweep in Facility for Joint FD
  5. FD Tenure should always be in months and days only.
  6. Authorize the request in RAP admin










  1. Handling of Rejects :-
    1. Action to rectify the rejections:-
      • In case TIN/PIN not validated - Supervisor to call up customer and request to revalidate TIN/PIN on IVR. supervisor to conference the customer call on IVR to validate
      • In case customer does not have sufficient balance in the account. Inform the rejection reason to the Agent who in turn will inform the same to the customer
      • In case of incorrect/incomplete details - supervisor to call up customer to get the correct/complete details
  2. Rejections by PhoneBanking Supervisor:- Supervisor to arrange to inform the rejection reason to the customer and update the customer intimation status in the summary file for future reference.
  3. Rejections by CPU:-  Supervisor to arrange to inform the rejection reason to the customer and update the customer intimation status in the summary file for future reference.
