TITLE   : HOTLISTING ON CALL PROCESS

DATED : 29th APRIL 2016

  • Call received at In-bound team for card hotlisting.
  • PhoneBanker to reconfirm if the request is for credit card hotlisting.
  • Also check if the credit card is in possession with the customer or not.


CARD IN POSSESSION PROCESS

  • Check if card is in possession with the customer (CIP - Yes) however the customer requests for the card to be hotlisted for any reasons then the hotlisting is to be actioned by the inbound PhoneBanker directly in CBCI using the appropriate hotlisting link
  • This call should not be transferred to the Hotlisting desk.
  • Please find the detailed process for the Hotlisting and reissue on call as shown below

NEVER RECEIVED WEB FORM FIELDS

 

  • PhoneBanker will need to update the appropriate details in the respective fields and click on the PROCESS tab.

  • In very rare scenarios the card may not get hotlisted in the system due to unexpected system errors such as system down, fluctuation etc. OR tblock is placed at the back end, however the response is not received in CBCI.

CARD BLOCKED AS REQUESTED

  • This will hotlist the card and Transfer the old card to a new card and relevant hot listing block code (L / S ) would be updated in the old card.
  • Basis the check for re-issue, N block will be automatically placed in the new card. N block will be placed on New card if any one of the below conditions is present -
  • Reissue field is selected as No
  • When Misused field is selected as Yes
  • Account is in a Block where re-issue is not permitted
  • Post this the PhoneBanker will be prompted for triggering request for hot listing, On clicking OK, the trigger for B24 hot listing will be initiated.






IMP Note :

 

When a customer calls PhoneBanking for Hotlisting & reissue of credit card which is in V or O block,PhoneBanker will update the reissue as "Yes" in the hotlisting link. the card gets hotlisted but the transferred card will be in "N" block. PhoneBanker to traverse to the transferred card account  & click on "Reissue on call " link to complete the reissue process


CARD NOT IN POSSESSION PROCESS

  • If card is not in possession with the customer (CIP - No), then the inbound PhoneBanker to take basic details such as name of caller, name of customer, card number (if available), mobile #, Cust id and access the card account in CBCI.
  • Post verifying if the caller is primary or add on holder, call to be Warm transferred to the Mumbai hotlisting desk. No level check verification is required to be done by the inbound PhoneBanker in this case as the verification will be done by the Hotlisting desk.


Talk off to the used with the customer–

Sir, Madam - I shall instantly transfer your call to my card blocking team, who shall block your card. In case the call gets disconnected kindly call us again. Please stay on line with me while I transfer your call...... (seek verbal nod/ acknowledgment before call transfer)

  • The details taken from the caller such as the name of caller, name of customer, card number (if available), mobile #, Cust id to be shared with the Hotlisting desk PhoneBanker.
  • Inbound PhoneBanker to update below sub category in CBCI on the call been received and transferred to the hotlisting desk.

CATGORY – HOTLISTING

SUB CATEGORY – UPDATE

Note – In the USER ID webform field the inbound PhoneBanker will need to enter the AD ID of Hotlisting desk PhoneBanker to whom the call was transferred.

  • On successful call transfer, the Hotlisting desk speaks to the customer and blocks the credit card in CBCI using the appropriate hotlisting link (& Base 24).
  • Hotlisting team to choose reissue as YES wherever applicable and customer to be informed about the reissue charges of Rs100/- and service tax applicable for reissue.


IMPORTANT

  • For these Hotlisting calls, in case of call drops / call disconnection - the team which was handling the call (whether inbound or hotlisting) will call out the customer to check and action hotlisting and appropriate system capture to be done on callout status.
  • If call out is done by the inbound team then the hotlisting will need to be actioned by them directly even if ‘CIP is no’ as the out bound call cannot be transferred to the hotlisting desk.
  • On blocking of the card, customer will receive a SMS on the card been blocked.
  • For cases where the inbound team is not able to transfer the call to the HL desk for various reasons, these cases must be actioned by the inbound team themselves so as to secure the card.
  • NO in reissue must be selected only for cases where the customer is not wanting a card to be reissued.
  • While selecting the reissue as YES, customer to be confirmed with mailing address to check on the card delivery. If customer claims of address change/modification, customer to be informed to send form for Card reissue form where the new mailing address can be updated and will also get updated in the CBCI. For Change of address cases, reissue to be selected as NO.
  • Customers who are calling PhoneBanking during Batch mode for hotlisting, PhoneBanker to take request for reissue along with the hotlisting as shown below:


  • Case will get move to exceptional queue after submitting and post approval of the case, card would be reissued.
  • During batch mode, reissue on call as stand alone link will not be functional.
  • Hence, the PhoneBankers to check whether the card needs to be reissued while card blocking and inform customer accordingly.



  • Customers who have Priority pass with the bank, whenever their credit card is blocked and reissued the priority pass will also be sent to the customers as per the current process. Customers to be informed about the same on every reissue call done on Infinia, Regalia, Diners, All Miles, Doctor Superia,etc. Priority pass will be issued only for the existing customers who already hold priority pass.
